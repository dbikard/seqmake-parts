P2 is a 29-bp σ70 constitutive promoter — by exact sequence the P2 promoter of the *E. coli* pBR322 *tet* region (a consensus −35 and a strong −10).

## Origin
The 29-bp sequence is an exact substring of the pBR322 *tet* promoter region (pBR322 positions ~1–38; Sutcliffe 1979 [PMID 383387](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/383387/)). Its −35/−10 elements and transcription start were characterized as a σ70 promoter by Harley et al. 1988 ([PMID 3045754](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/3045754/)); the consensus-promoter architecture follows Lutz & Bujard 1997 ([PMID 9092630](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/9092630/)).

## Properties
- **−35 box** [0:6] TTGACA — perfect σ70 −35 consensus.
- **−10 box** [23:29] TTTAAT — strong −10 variant (5/6 to TATAAT), 17 bp downstream of the −35.
- Strong, constitutive σ70 promoter.

## Use
A short constitutive σ70 promoter for *E. coli*. As delimited it ends at the −10; extend the 3′ boundary to include the +1 transcription start before use as an expression promoter.
