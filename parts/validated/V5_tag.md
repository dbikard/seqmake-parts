# V5_tag

A 14-amino-acid epitope tag (GKPIPNPLLGLDST) derived from the phosphoprotein of Parainfluenza virus 5 / Simian virus 5 (PIV5/SV5), recognized with sub-nanomolar affinity by the SV5-Pk1 (Pk) monoclonal antibody and widely used for protein detection, purification, and imaging.

## Origin

The V5 tag corresponds to residues 95–108 of the P/V phosphoprotein of Parainfluenza virus 5 (formerly Simian virus 5, strain W3; [UniProt P11208](https://www.uniprot.org/uniprotkb/P11208/)). The epitope was first defined through monoclonal antibodies raised against the SV5 P protein ([PMID 2445904](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/2445904/)), and the minimal binding determinant was mapped by synthetic-peptide competition to a core (IPNPLLGLD) containing a beta-turn motif essential for antibody recognition ([PMID 1713260](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/1713260/)). The 14-residue oligopeptide GKPIPNPLLGLDST was then established as a portable "Pk" tag that retains antigenicity when fused to recombinant proteins ([PMID 1372038](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/1372038/)). The sequence has been unchanged in the reviewed Swiss-Prot record since its original publication.

## Properties

- **Length:** 14 aa (42 bp coding), small enough to minimize perturbation of the tagged protein.
- **Antibody affinity:** the murine SV5-Pk1 monoclonal antibody binds the tag with KD ~441 pM; a humanized anti-V5 antibody (Hu6) binds with KD ~572 pM ([PMID 40723447](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/40723447/), [PMID 39786361](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/39786361/)).
- **Epitope core:** the minimal antibody-recognition determinant is IPNPLLGLD, with a beta-turn motif required for binding ([PMID 1713260](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/1713260/)).
- **Stability:** the tag lacks arginine and shows low hydrophobicity, conferring reported trypsin resistance and favorable solubility for tagged fusions ([PMID 40723447](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/40723447/)).

## Use

- **Detection and imaging:** immunohistochemistry on FFPE tissue and quantitative flow cytometry using anti-V5 antibodies ([PMID 40723447](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/40723447/)).
- **Affinity purification:** antibody-based capture of tagged fusions, including dual-tag (His + Pk) purification schemes and solid matrix-antibody-antigen complex assembly ([PMID 1372038](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/1372038/), [PMID 7504859](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/7504859/)).
- **Cell engineering:** CAR-T cell tracking and gene-therapy monitoring via surface or intracellular V5 detection ([PMID 40723447](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/40723447/)).
- **Recombinant production:** humanized anti-V5 antibody produced from single-cycle PIV5 vectors at 20–50 mg/L for therapeutic and synthetic-biology applications ([PMID 39786361](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/39786361/)).
