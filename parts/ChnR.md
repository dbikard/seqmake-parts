# ChnR — cyclohexanone-responsive activator

AraC/XylS-family transcriptional activator that drives the **PchnB** promoter in
response to cyclohexanone — the cognate regulator that makes PchnB inducible.

## Origin
ChnR is part of the cyclohexanol/cyclohexanone degradation (*chn*) gene cluster of
*Acinetobacter johnsonii* (NCIMB 9871). Sequence: the 942 bp (313 aa) *chnR* CDS
from GenBank [AB006902.2](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/nuccore/AB006902.2).

## Properties
- 942 bp CDS, 313 aa, AraC/XylS-family (DNA-binding + effector domains).
- Activates transcription from PchnB upon binding cyclohexanone.

## Use
Pair with the **PchnB** promoter for cyclohexanone-inducible expression in
*E. coli* and related hosts.
